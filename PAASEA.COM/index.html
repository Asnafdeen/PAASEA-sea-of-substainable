About PAASEA

PAASEA is a pioneering initiative transforming India's coastal potential into a circular, climate-resilient economy. We cultivate sustainably farmed seaweed to develop eco-friendly bioplastics, organic biofertilizers, and nutrient-rich products — creating scalable solutions to reduce plastic waste, improve soil health, and support sustainable industries.


More than a green tech venture, PAASEA is a social innovation movement working hand-in-hand with coastal fisherwomen and rural communities to create livelihood opportunities and equitable growth.

Our Seaweed Innovations

Harnessing the ocean's potential to create products that heal the planet.

🌿 Seaweed Bioplastic

Traditional plastics pollute oceans for centuries. Our seaweed bioplastic decomposes naturally, offering a sustainable packaging alternative made from sustainably harvested seaweed.

🌾 Organic Seaweed Fertilizer

Modern agriculture damages soil health. Our seaweed fertilizer restores nutrients naturally while boosting crop productivity — grown with minimal environmental impact.

🥗 Seaweed Nutrition

Seaweed is rich in minerals, antioxidants, and essential nutrients. Our nutrition products bring the power of the ocean to healthy, everyday diets.

Our Solutions

Building a sustainable future through seaweed innovation and community empowerment.

🚺 Empowering Fisherwomen

We create livelihood opportunities for coastal fisherwomen by integrating them into the seaweed value chain — with training, support, and fair market access.

🌍 Reducing Plastic Pollution

Our seaweed-based bioplastics replace harmful single-use plastics and help protect oceans and ecosystems for generations to come.

🌊 Supporting Blue Economy

By cultivating seaweed sustainably, we strengthen India's coastal economy and promote marine sustainability across the value chain.
